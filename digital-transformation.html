Focus Area

Digital Transformation

Technology-enabled financial infrastructure

Thesis

The Infrastructure of Tomorrow's Economy

The financial services industry is being fundamentally reshaped by technology. From how value is stored and transferred to how risk is assessed and managed, the infrastructure underpinning economic activity is being rebuilt.

We are not passive observers of this transformation. Through strategic investments and direct involvement in building fintech ventures, we participate in shaping the architecture of future financial systems.

Our interest is not in technology for its own sake, but in solutions that solve real problems, platforms that make financial services more accessible, efficient, and resilient.

Areas of Interest

Where We Focus

Payment Infrastructure: The systems that move value between parties, domestically and across borders. As the Gulf integrates further into global commerce, efficient, compliant payment rails become critical infrastructure.

Enterprise Financial Systems: Platforms that enable businesses to manage financial operations with greater efficiency, transparency, and control. The digitisation of corporate finance is still in early stages.

Regulatory Technology: Solutions that help financial institutions meet compliance obligations efficiently. As regulatory frameworks mature across the GCC, demand for sophisticated regtech grows proportionally.

Financial Data Infrastructure: The systems that aggregate, analyse, and present financial information to enable better decisions, for institutions, enterprises, and individuals.

Our Approach

Builder and Investor

We engage with digital transformation in two ways:

As builders: We have direct involvement in developing fintech solutions, contributing capital, strategic direction, and access to our network. These are ventures where we have meaningful influence over outcomes.

As investors: We take strategic positions in technology companies whose work aligns with our thesis on the future of financial infrastructure. These are typically growth-stage businesses with proven models seeking expansion capital.

In both cases, we bring more than capital. Our relationships across the Gulf, with financial institutions, regulators, and enterprises, create value for the ventures we support.

Regional Context

The GCC Opportunity

The Gulf presents a unique environment for financial technology. Young, digitally-native populations. Governments actively modernising regulatory frameworks. Established financial institutions seeking technology partnerships. Significant capital seeking deployment.

We believe the region is positioned not merely to adopt financial technology developed elsewhere, but to become a centre of innovation, developing solutions that address local needs and can scale globally.

Our role is to support that evolution: connecting entrepreneurs with capital, technology with distribution, and innovation with the institutional relationships required for meaningful scale.

Building Something Significant?

We engage selectively with founders and teams building technology that reshapes financial infrastructure. If there may be alignment, we welcome a conversation.
