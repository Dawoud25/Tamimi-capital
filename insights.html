Building a Knowledge Economy in the UAE

In January 2024, D Al Tamimi Capital participated in the Changers Club, where our Director delivered a keynote address on the transformative potential of building a knowledge-based economy in the UAE.

The address explored the UAE's strategic advantages as a global hub, the compelling opportunity for European and international businesses relocating to the region, and the essential role of innovation in sustaining long-term prosperity.
