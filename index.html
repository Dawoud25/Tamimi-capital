Who We Are

Guided by Legacy,
Driven by Architecture

D Al Tamimi Capital is the principal investment platform of a privately held Gulf based family office, operating at the intersection of capital architecture, institutional continuity, and nationally aligned innovation.

With deep regional roots and a long-term orientation, we engage selectively in initiatives that strengthen the region's position as a global destination for capital, enterprise, and talent.

Our activities span the structuring of institutional foreign direct investment platforms, the development of intergenerational governance and continuity frameworks for family enterprises, and the origination of technology ventures aligned with national economic priorities.

We invest with patience. We commit for the long term. We operate with absolute discretion.

Our Principles

Our Distinction

Heritage Meets
Modern Architecture

Our distinction lies in operating at the sovereign-institutional nexus. We work alongside global financial institutions, and leading operating partners to design structures that are globally competitive, locally grounded, and built for endurance. Every approach is defined by a unique blend of heritage, expertise, and institutional rigour. As a trusted partner, we bring depth to every collaboration.

Heritage of Excellence: Rooted in the legacy of a distinguished Gulf family, we design structures that are globally competitive, locally grounded, and aligned with national economic priorities.

Institutional Architecture: We build platforms and frameworks, not just execute transactions, with focus on scale, governance, and global competitiveness.

Generational Continuity: Leveraging relationships with sovereign entities and leading institutions, we provide access to capital and exclusive opportunities. Our work spans generations, focusing on governance, succession, and institutional resilience.

Innovation Origination: We originate technology ventures that demonstrate how globally competitive innovation can be conceived, built, and scaled from the UAE, reflecting our commitment to meaningful impact and lasting value.
