Who We Are

Guided by Legacy.
Driven by Architecture.

D Al Tamimi Capital is the principal investment and structuring platform of a privately held Gulf-based family office, operating at the intersection of capital architecture, institutional continuity, and nationally aligned innovation.

With deep regional roots and a long-term orientation, we engage selectively in initiatives that strengthen the region's position as a global destination for institutional capital, enterprise, and talent.

Our activities include the design and structuring of institutional-grade foreign direct investment platforms, the development of intergenerational governance and continuity frameworks for family enterprises, and the origination of select technology ventures aligned with national economic priorities.

Across all initiatives, we prioritise scale, governance, and long-term alignment, working alongside institutional capital partners and Tier-1 operating counterparts.

Our Pillars

Capital & Institutional Architecture

We design and structure institutional-grade platforms for capital formation and foreign direct investment into the UAE. Our work focuses on aligning global investor requirements with local regulatory frameworks, national development objectives, and long-term value creation. We collaborate selectively with global financial institutions, asset managers, and Tier-1 operating partners to build structures that are globally competitive and locally grounded.

Enterprise Continuity

Family-owned enterprises remain central to the region's economic fabric, yet many face challenges transitioning beyond the first and second generation. Through targeted initiatives, applied research, and enabling frameworks, we engage in strengthening governance, succession planning, and institutional resilience. Our approach is long-term and systems-oriented, supporting continuity across generations rather than individual cycles.

Innovation Origination

We originate and support technology ventures that demonstrate how globally competitive innovation can be conceived, built, and scaled from the UAE. Our focus is on initiatives aligned with national economic priorities, including the UAE's D33 agenda, with an emphasis on regulatory integrity, real-world applicability, and durable impact.

Stewardship & Governance

We approach every initiative with a stewardship mindset, prioritising governance, alignment, and long-term responsibility. Our structures are designed to meet institutional standards while remaining adaptable to evolving regulatory, economic, and technological environments.
