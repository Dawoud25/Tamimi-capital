Who We Are

Guided by Legacy,
Driven by Vision

D Al Tamimi Capital is the strategic investment arm of a distinguished Gulf based family office. Rooted in generations of regional engagement, we partner with institutional investors to deploy capital across private credit, commercial real estate, and transformative enterprise.

Our position at the intersection of heritage and innovation allows us to identify opportunities that others may overlook, while our institutional discipline ensures that every engagement meets the highest standards of governance and due diligence.
