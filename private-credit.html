Overview

Capital Solutions for the Gulf's Evolution

The Gulf region is undergoing an unprecedented economic transformation. As traditional financing channels evolve and institutional capital seeks exposure to the region's growth, we position ourselves at the intersection of opportunity and execution.

Our focus spans structured private credit solutions and premium commercial real estate assets across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, and select Gulf markets. We work exclusively with institutional investors, family offices, and professional counterparties who share our long term perspective.

Private Credit

Structured Financing Solutions

We structure and facilitate private credit transactions for established enterprises and significant projects seeking capital beyond traditional banking channels. Our approach emphasises rigorous structuring, appropriate security, and alignment of interests across all parties.

Our activities include:

• Senior and subordinated debt structures
• Mezzanine financing solutions
• Asset-backed lending arrangements
• Project and infrastructure financing

Each engagement is evaluated on its fundamental merits, the quality of counterparties, and the robustness of the underlying structure. We do not pursue volume, we pursue transactions where our participation adds genuine value.

Commercial Real Estate

Premium Assets,
Institutional Standards

Our commercial real estate practice focuses on premium assets in established and emerging nodes across the Gulf. We identify opportunities with compelling fundamentals for institutional and professional investors seeking stable, long term returns with regional exposure.

Geographic focus:

• United Arab Emirates , Dubai, Abu Dhabi
• Kingdom of Saudi Arabia , Riyadh, Jeddah, NEOM corridor
• Select opportunities across Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman

Asset classes:

• Grade A commercial office
• Logistics and industrial
• Retail and mixed-use
• Hospitality assets

Through our regional network and on-the-ground presence, we source opportunities, including off-market transactions, that may not be accessible through conventional channels.

Our Approach

How We Engage

Institutional Standards: Every engagement adheres to institutional-grade due diligence, documentation, and governance regardless of transaction size.

Alignment of Interests: We structure arrangements where all parties share appropriate risk and reward. Our reputation depends on outcomes, not fees.

Long-term Relationships: We measure success over years and decades, not quarters. Our institutional partners return because we deliver on commitments.

Discretion: The details of our engagements remain confidential. What appears here represents our areas of activity, not a catalog of transactions.
